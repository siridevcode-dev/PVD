OUR SERVICES

Integrated Resettlement Service Model with LSTK Approach

Core Service Philosophy

Lump Sum Turnkey (LSTK) Model

We provide comprehensive Lump Sum Turnkey (LSTK) management of Environment Management Programs and Resettlement Action Plans, taking full responsibility for delivering successful outcomes from planning through post-relocation support.

Integrated Service Process

Two-Phase Approach to Success

1

Phase 1: Planning & Compliance

Risk Management & Foundation Setting

RAP Finalization & Compliance Review

Census, Socio-Economic Surveys & Inventory of Losses (IOL)

Public Consultation & Stakeholder Engagement

Grievance Redress Mechanism (GRM) Setup

2

Phase 2: Execution & Transition

Livelihood Security & Community Building

Compensation & Entitlement Distribution

Physical Relocation Support

Housing/Site Provision (Serviced land/fully built houses)

Post-Relocation Support (3-5 years LIRP continuation)

Transparency & Accountability

We ensure open communication and clear accountability mechanisms throughout all projects.

Grievance Redress Mechanism (GRM)

We maintain a transparent process for submitting, tracking, and resolving community grievances, ensuring trust and accountability.

  • Clear submission process for complaints
  • Tracking system with timeline commitments
  • Transparent resolution communication
  • Community awareness campaigns

Monitoring & Audit

Continuous internal and external monitoring throughout project implementation, leading to a final Completion Audit for lenders and stakeholders.

  • Regular internal progress reviews
  • External independent monitoring
  • Lender compliance reporting
  • Final Completion Audit certification
